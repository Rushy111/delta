About Umbrafiles

We believe in digital sovereignty.

Umbrafiles was born from a simple frustration: every time you want to save a file online, you hand over your data to someone else's server. We built Umbrafiles to change that. Your files belong to you — and they should stay that way.

🎯

Our Mission

To give every internet user a simple, beautiful, and private way to archive their files without compromising their privacy. No sign-ups, no cloud dependency, no compromises.

🛡️

Our Promise

We will never store your files on our servers. We will never sell your data. We will never track you. Umbrafiles is a static website — there's nothing to hack, nothing to steal.

🔧

Our Technology

Built entirely with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Your files are stored in your browser's IndexedDB — a powerful, built-in database that works offline and requires no external services.

🌍

Our Philosophy

The internet was meant to be a place of freedom. Umbrafiles is a small step toward reclaiming that freedom — one file at a time. No corporate agendas, no venture capital, just a tool that works.

📖

The Name

"Umbra" refers to the darkest part of a shadow. Just as a shadow is always with you but never intrusive, Umbrafiles is always there for your files — present when you need it, invisible when you don't.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

At Umbrafiles, privacy isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation. We built this Service because we believe your data is yours alone. This Privacy Policy explains how Umbrafiles handles (and, more importantly, does not handle) your information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Umbrafiles is designed with a privacy-first architecture. Specifically, we:

  • 🚫 Do not collect any personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • 🚫 Do not require account creation or login
  • 🚫 Do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising
  • 🚫 Do not employ third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts
  • 🚫 Do not store any files on our servers (because we don't have servers for this purpose)
  • 🚫 Do not transmit any of your data to any external service
  • 🚫 Do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • 🚫 Do not track your browsing behavior or device fingerprinting

3. Information Stored Locally

Umbrafiles uses your browser's built-in storage technologies:

  • IndexedDB: Your uploaded files are stored here. This is your browser's local database. No data leaves your device.
  • localStorage: We store your theme preference (dark/light) here. No personal data is stored.
  • Cookies: Umbrafiles does not set any cookies. If your browser stores any cookies, they are not set by Umbrafiles.

4. Your Files

Your files are stored exclusively on your device. We want to be absolutely clear about this:

  • Your files never leave your browser
  • No file metadata is transmitted anywhere
  • No file content is processed by any server
  • You have complete control — view, download, or delete at any time

If you clear your browser's site data or storage, your files will be permanently deleted. We recommend using the Export All feature to maintain backups.

5. Security

While we take the following measures seriously:

  • The Service is a static website with no server-side code to exploit
  • All data resides in your browser's IndexedDB, which is sandboxed by your browser
  • No network requests are made for data transmission

We acknowledge that no system is 100% secure. If you're concerned about security, consider the nature of browser-based storage and ensure your device itself is secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Umbrafiles does not integrate with any third-party services that could access your data. The only external resource we load is the Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts from Google Fonts, which is a standard web font delivery service. Loading these fonts does not constitute data collection by Umbrafiles.

7. Children's Privacy

Umbrafiles is available to users of all ages. However, we encourage parents and guardians to guide younger users in understanding local data storage and the importance of digital backups.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the Umbrafiles website. We're committed to transparency and will respond to all privacy-related inquiries.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or process personal data, your rights are straightforward:

  • You can view all your stored data in your browser's developer tools
  • You can delete all your data by clearing your browser storage
  • You can export all your files at any time using our Export feature
  • You can stop using Umbrafiles at any time — no account to cancel
